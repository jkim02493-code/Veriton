Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What We Collect

We collect your email address, account plan type, and search usage count when you create an account.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not store the contents of your Google Docs. We only process selected text temporarily to find academic sources. This text is never saved to our servers.

How We Use Your Data

Your email is used for login and account management. Your usage count is used to enforce free and pro plan limits.

Third Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. We use Stripe for payment processing. We use Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, and Crossref to find academic sources.
