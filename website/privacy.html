Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What We Collect

Veriton collects only the account and usage information needed to provide the service:

  • Email address: Used for login, account identification, and important account notices.
  • Plan type: Used to identify whether your account is on the Free or Pro plan.
  • Usage count: Used to enforce search limits for Free and Pro accounts.

What We Do Not Collect

Veriton does not store the contents of your Google Docs. Document text or selected claim text may be processed temporarily to find academic sources, but the full contents of your Google Docs are not saved by Veriton.

Chrome Extension Permissions

Veriton requests only the Chrome extension permissions needed for the product to work:

  • activeTab: Used to access the current Google Docs tab and read the current document context when you choose to use Veriton.
  • storage: Used to save your login session locally in Chrome so you do not need to sign in every time.
  • identity: Used for Google OAuth login and authentication.

How We Use Your Data

Your email is used for login and account management. Your plan type and usage count are used to enforce Free and Pro plan limits and keep your account in sync across sessions.

Third Party Services

Veriton uses the following third party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and account data storage.
  • Stripe: Payment processing and subscription management.
  • Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, and Crossref: Academic source retrieval.

Data Retention

Account and usage data is retained while your account is active. If you delete your account or request deletion, Veriton will delete associated account data within 30 days, unless retention is required by law or needed for fraud prevention, billing, or security purposes.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account data by emailing support@veriton.app. We may need to verify your identity before completing a deletion request.

Children's Privacy

Veriton is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Policy Changes

If this Privacy Policy changes in a material way, Veriton will notify users by email.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests? Email us at support@veriton.app.
